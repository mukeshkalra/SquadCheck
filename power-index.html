Methodology

How we rank every team

Four weighted factors. Every player scored fairly by position. No opinion, no bias.

01
Start with the squad
We take the predicted 26-man roster for each team, based on recent call-ups, qualifying squads, and expert projections. When FIFA finalises official squads on May 26, we update instantly.
Position-relative percentiles
02
Rate every player — fairly, by position
A world-class goalkeeper shouldn't be penalised for not scoring goals. We compare each player against others in their position using market value as a baseline, then adjust for current season form.
34,000+ player database
03
Reward what matters now
Market value tells you who was good last year. We layer in this season's goals, assists, and club performance to capture who's in form right now — and who's fading.
Current season data
04
Build the picture
A team's score comes from four things: how good their best XI is (50%), how deep their bench is (20%), how many young stars could break out (15%), and recent international results (15%).
Multi-factor index
Player Rating = Market Value Percentile×0.60 + Form×0.25 + Age×0.05 + Club Strength×0.10
SC Power Index = Squad Quality×0.50 + X-Factor×0.15 + Squad Depth×0.20 + Recent Form×0.15
